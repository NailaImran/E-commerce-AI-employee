Written by Brooke Reotutar, Senior Content Producer @ LinkedIn for Marketing Remember when the internet was like a sprawling library? You would walk the aisles, searching the shelves on your own until you finally found what you needed. That era is ending. Now, with AI-led discovery, you simply voice your question to a librarian, and, almost instantly, the answers emerge at your fingertips — curated, concise, and ready for your next move. Publishers used to compete for the best shelf placement to catch your eye. Now, the real competition is convincing the librarian — the AI — that your book is the one worth recommending. There is a science to winning that recommendation. Ensuring that AI systems trust and surface your brand’s content requires intentional effort. You need to focus on specific areas: Readability Clear structure Technical elements Media optimization LinkedIn just published an in-depth guide on how to get discovered in this new landscape.
