# Email Reply Templates

Use these as starting points. Always personalise with order details from vault files.

## order_query — Order Status Question

```
Dear [Name],

Thank you for reaching out! Your order [#XXXX] has been received and is currently
[being processed / packed and ready to dispatch / on its way to you].

You can expect delivery within [X business days]. We'll send a tracking email
as soon as your order ships.

Please don't hesitate to contact us if you have any other questions.

Best regards,
[Store Name] Team
```

## shipping_query — Shipping / Address Question

```
Dear [Name],

Thank you for your message. Regarding your order [#XXXX]:

[Answer the specific shipping question — estimated date, tracking number, address confirmation]

If you need to update your shipping address and your order hasn't shipped yet,
please reply immediately and we'll do our best to help.

Best regards,
[Store Name] Team
```

## complaint — Customer Complaint

```
Dear [Name],

Thank you for bringing this to our attention, and we sincerely apologise for
the inconvenience you've experienced with your order [#XXXX].

[Acknowledge the specific issue — wrong item / damaged / not as described]

We want to make this right for you. Here's what we'll do:
[Specific resolution — replacement / refund / partial credit]

Please [reply with a photo / confirm your address] so we can process this
as quickly as possible.

Again, we're sorry this happened and appreciate your patience.

Best regards,
[Store Name] Team
```

## refund_request — Refund or Return

```
Dear [Name],

Thank you for contacting us about your order [#XXXX]. We understand your
concern and want to help.

Our return process:
1. Please [return the item / confirm the issue with a photo]
2. Refunds are processed within 5-7 business days once approved
3. You'll receive a confirmation email when your refund is initiated

[If eligible]: We've initiated a review of your refund request and will
follow up within 24 hours.

Best regards,
[Store Name] Team
```

## general — General Inquiry

```
Dear [Name],

Thank you for reaching out to [Store Name]!

[Answer the specific question as concisely as possible]

If there's anything else we can help you with, please don't hesitate to ask.

Best regards,
[Store Name] Team
```
